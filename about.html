Our Story

NAMRIAkbay was born out of a vision to democratize access to geospatial information. Recognizing the crucial role that accurate and up-to-date geographical data plays in various sectors—from urban planning and disaster management to environmental conservation and beyond—we embarked on a mission to create a platform that would make this data accessible and useful to all.

Since our inception, we have been committed to innovation, accuracy, and user-centric design. Our journey has been driven by a passion for leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and empower communities with the information they need to make informed decisions.
