Frequently Asked Question


Here are some of the most questions we received.

What is a 360-degree virtual tour?

Using panoramic photography, a 360-degree virtual tour is an interactive experience that lets viewers explore a place or environment from every angle. Users are able to move around the area as though they were physically there.

How do I navigate through the virtual tour?

To navigate around the tour, use the arrow icons to go up, down, left, or right. As an alternative, you may navigate by clicking and dragging the screen or by using the arrow keys on your keyboard.
Also refer to the Virtual Guide above for more information.

What is VR mode and how do I activate it?

With a VR headset, you may enjoy the virtual tour in VR mode for a completely immersive experience. To enable VR mode, click the icon and follow the on-screen directions.

What are these hotspot buttons?

An interactive component in web design, a hotspot button performs particular functions when clicked or hovered over. By making interfaces more dynamic and facilitating simple access to relevant information, it improves the user experience. Polygon hotspots draw attention to particular places, information hotspots give multimedia material, and quick hotspots allow instant access to crucial information.

Refer to the Virtual Guide manual above for more information.

How can I share the virtual tour?

By selecting the "share" icon, you may send the virtual tour to others. You may then choose to share via email, social media, or by copying the tour's website.
